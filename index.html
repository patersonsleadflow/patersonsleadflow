Who We Are

Paterson’s Lead Flow is a specialist lead generation company dedicated to roofing businesses. We understand the unique challenges roofing companies face in finding qualified, local customers who genuinely need roofing inspections, repairs, or replacements.

Our mission is to deliver exclusive, high-quality leads that connect roofing companies directly with homeowners ready to move forward. We don’t just provide contact information — we deliver vetted prospects and confirmed appointments, so companies can focus on closing jobs instead of chasing cold leads.

What We Provide

  • Qualified leads tailored to your business needs
  • Consistent monthly lead delivery
  • Expert customer support and personalised service
  • Transparent tracking and reporting
  • Exclusive coverage in Edinburgh, Scottish Borders, and Fife

How It Started

Paterson’s Lead Flow was founded by Connor Paterson to support his father’s roofing company during the winter months. Starting as a family-driven effort, the company quickly grew its expertise in generating highly qualified, exclusive leads tailored specifically for roofing businesses.

Today, Paterson’s Lead Flow helps roofing companies across Edinburgh, the Scottish Borders, and Fife by delivering pre-qualified, local homeowners ready for inspections, repairs, or full roof replacements. By focusing on quality over quantity, the business ensures clients receive real opportunities that convert into booked jobs, saving time and maximising revenue.
